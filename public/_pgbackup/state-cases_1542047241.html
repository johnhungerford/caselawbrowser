Caselaw Browser

American Caselaw

A simple app to browse, search, and access a comprehensive database of American caselaw. This app is powered by the Caselaw Access Project (CAP) API developed by the Library Innovation Lab at Harvard University and Ravel Law.

Federal Cases

Click here to search through federal-level case law. Includes Supreme Court cases as well as cases from all other federal courts. Search by decision date, court, and full-text search terms.

State Cases

Click here to search through all non-federal case law. This includes all state, tribal, and territorial jurisdictions, past and present, going back to the 17th century. Search by jurisdiction, court, decision date, and full-text search terms.
